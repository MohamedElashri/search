Bang Patterns - You can add a shortcode prefixed with '!' to the start or end of your query to quickly switch to a specific search engine or website.


Snap Patterns - You can add a shortcode prefixed with '@' to the start or end of your query to do a search in your default engine that is filtered to a specific site.
